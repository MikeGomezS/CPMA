Project Management

Descrition

We offer comprehensive project management from conceptualization to final delivery, ensuring proper planning, coordination, and control at every stage. Our approach guarantees the fulfillment of objectives, budgets, deadlines, and quality standards, providing certainty and value to each development.

Key activities:

- Definition of scope, objectives, and strategic guidelines.
- Development of schedules, workflows, and responsibility matrices.
- Interdisciplinary coordination between architects, engineers, contractors, and consultants.
- Continuous supervision and control of progress, budget, and performance.
- Closure and final delivery with complete and validated technical documentation.

Pre-construction

Description

Key stage of strategic planning and comprehensive analysis, focused on ensuring the technical, economic, and legal viability of the project before construction begins. During this phase, we lay the groundwork for efficient, safe, and profitable development.

Key activities:

- Technical, financial, and site feasibility studies.
- Cost estimates and detailed budgets for informed decision-making.
- Definition of construction and logistics strategies that optimize resources and time.
- Early coordination with designers, specialists, and consultants.
- Design optimization aimed at reducing costs, risks, and execution times.

Construction Management

Description

- Attention to and resolution of unforeseen events or adjustments on site.

BIM (Building Information Modeling) Administration

Description

We implement BIM (Building Information Modeling) methodology for intelligent, collaborative, and accurate project management, integrating all technical, structural, and facility information into 3D digital models. This tool allows us to optimize processes, anticipate conflicts, and improve decision-making at every stage of the project lifecycle.

Key activities:

- Collaborative 3D modeling between design, engineering, and construction disciplines.
- Detection and resolution of interferences through coordinated model review.
- Automated generation of updated technical documentation.
- Simulation and digital planning of construction phases.
- Continuous management of BIM databases, from design to building operation.

Infrastructure

Description

We develop civil and urban infrastructure projects using a comprehensive approach that encompasses the design, management, and construction of works essential to regional development. We apply high technical and sustainability standards to ensure operational efficiency, durability, and long-term value.

Key activities:

Renovation and Maintenance

Construction Supervision

Description

We carry out technical, administrative, and quality control supervision during the execution of the work, ensuring strict compliance with plans, specifications, regulations, and international standards. Our comprehensive on-site monitoring guarantees efficient, safe execution in accordance with the project objectives.

Key activities:

- Ongoing inspection of physical progress and quality verification.
- Monitoring compliance with construction programs, schedules, and procedures.
- Supervision of measurements, estimates, and valuations.
- Issuance of technical reports, evidence, and construction documentation.
- Management and technical resolution of incidents during execution.
