Project Management

Descrition

Project management from conceptualization to final delivery. Includes strategic planning, coordination between disciplines, budget control, risk management, deadline compliance, and quality standards.

Key activities:

- Definition of scope and objectives.
- Development of schedules and workflows.
- Coordination of architects, engineers, and contractors.
- Continuous monitoring of project progress and performance.
- Final delivery with complete documentation.

Pre-construction

Description

Early planning phase that ensures the technical, economic, and legal feasibility of the project before work begins.

Key activities:

- Feasibility studies and site analysis.
- Cost estimates and detailed budgets.
- Development of constructive and logistical strategies.
- Coordination with designers and specialists.
- Design optimization to reduce costs and time.

Construction Management

Description

Direct supervision of the execution of the work in the field, ensuring that specifications, regulations, and quality standards are met.

Key activities:

- Quality control of materials and work.
- Contractor and supplier management.
- Industrial safety and civil protection.
- Resolution of unforeseen events and changes on site.
- Progress reports and milestone compliance.

BIM (Building Information Modeling) Administration

Description

Use of BIM methodology for intelligent project management using 3D digital models that integrate technical, structural, and facility information.

Key activities:

- Collaborative 3D modeling.
- Detection of interference with other disciplines.
- Generation of automated technical documentation.
- Simulation of construction phases.
- Maintenance of an updated database throughout the project lifecycle.

Infrastructure

Description

Design, management, and construction of civil and urban infrastructure projects.

Key activities:

- Earthworks and site preparation.

Renovation and Maintenance

Description

Renovation, modernization, and maintenance of existing buildings and facilities, whether due to wear and tear, technological upgrades, or change of use.

Key activities:

- Diagnosis of structural and functional conditions.
- Planning interventions with minimal operational impact.
- Renovation of facades, facilities, and interior spaces.
- Preventive and corrective maintenance of MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) systems.
- Permit management and coordination with users during construction.

Construction Supervision

Description

Continuous and technical supervision of the execution of the work to ensure compliance with plans, specifications, regulations, and quality standards..

Key activities:

- Daily inspection of progress and quality.
- Verification of program and sequence compliance.
- Measurement and valuation control.
- Photographic documentation and progress reports.
- Technical troubleshooting in the field.
